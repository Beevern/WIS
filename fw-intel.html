    How to Use

    EVE D-Scan Pilot Matcher

    This tool helps low-sec PvP-ers match ships on d-scan to pilots in local to see their fit.

    Best Practices

    It works best when there are not too many pilots, as that will make determining who is flying what more difficult. Long pastes will also increase analyzing time.

    Therefore, reducing the angle/range of d-scan and using station paste to remove docked pilots will help lots.

    Paste Screen

    There are 4 inputs: d-scan, local, station guest list (optional), screenshot of overview (optional).

    D-Scan & Local

    Simply copy and paste. These are the most important pastes for scans.

    Guest List

    An optional paste of the station guest list to remove capsuleers who are docked from analyzation since they aren't in space.

    Screenshot

    A screenshot of overview that confirms pilots' presence and matches them to a ship. In the case that the pilot name is parsed incorrectly, you can edit it.

    You can do a screenshot-only scan to see their fits quickly.

    Results Screen

    A list of ships (in alphabetical order) will be displayed.

    • The number of each ship and number of pilot matches are displayed on the right.
    • Beside the ship name, there may be orange and/or green indicators to convey certain information:
      • Green "Match confirmed" shows how many pilots have been matched to that ship (through screenshot)
      • Orange "On grid" shows how many of this ship is on grid (through d-scan)
    • Base resistances of that ship are shown.
    • A list of capsuleers who have previously used/lost this ship will be displayed:
      • In order of activity, with most recent activity at the top
      • If that pilot was confirmed through screenshot, a green indicator will be beside their name
      • For each capsuleer, their name, corp, alliance, faction, date/value/fit of last lost are displayed
      • Hovering over the fit will provide a visual representation
      • Clicking it will link to the zKill page
